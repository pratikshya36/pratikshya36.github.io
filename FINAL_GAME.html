CHERRY JACKPOT GAME

INSTRUCTIONS TO PLAY THE GAME

  • CLICK SPIN TO START THE SPIN
  • CLICK STOP TO STOP THE GAME AND KNOW THE FINAL POINTS
  • CLICK RESTART TO RESTART THE GAME
  • IF THE NUMBERS ON THE THREE BOXES ARE NOT SAME POINT IS DEDUCTED
  • IF THE NUMBERS ON THE THREE BOXES ARE SAME POINT IS ADDED
